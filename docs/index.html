Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Speed Reader application ("the App") developed by HTB.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Speed Reader is designed with your privacy as the top priority. The App operates entirely on your device (locally) and does not require you to create an account, nor does it track your usage or collect telemetry.

2. Local File Access & Web Articles

When you use the App to read documents, the files are accessed completely locally on your device. For the "Web Article Support" feature, the App makes a direct HTTP request from your device to the website URL you provide in order to download and format the article text locally. At no point is this URL, or any downloaded text, sent back to us or any third-party analytics server.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party tracking, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting software. The Text-to-Speech (TTS) feature utilizes your device's native built-in speech engine.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes that involve collecting data in the future, we will notify you through an update to the App and update this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at hassanteslim12@gmail.com.
